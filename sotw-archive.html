Sophie's New Sandwich

Student of the Week from April 28 – May 2, 2014

7 Yumms

Ingredients:

2 slices of white bread

1 avocado

1 lettuce leaf

1 tomato

3 slices of pickles

100 g of chicken

ketchup

Directions:

1 – Cook 100 g of chicken.

2 – Wash the tomato, avocado, and lettuce.

3 – Put one slice of bread on your favourite plate.

4 – Mash the avocado and spread it on the bread.

5 – Place one lettuce leaf on the avocado.

6 – Cut the tomato into slices and put on top of the lettuce.

7 – Place three slices of pickles on top of the tomato.

8 – Place the cooked chicken on top of the pickles.

9 – Spread the ketchup over the chicken.

10 – Take the second slice of bread and place it on top of the chicken and ketchup.

11 – Enjoy the “7 Yumms” sandwich.

How to Build a Wooden Airplane

Would you like to build cool projects like birdhouses, wooden airplanes, fire trucks, or even more?

Home Depot offers many interesting workshops for kids of different ages. I attended the Fire Rescue Workshop with my mom and received an orange apron. There were five children of ages five to twelve and an instructor who helped us build an airplane.

There are six steps to make a wooden airplane.

Step 1: Hammer the top wing.

Step 2: Hammer the propeller into the body of the airplane.

Step 3: Glue the horizontal part of the tail into the back of the plane.

Step 4: Glue the vertical part of the wing to the back of the plane.

Step 5: Put the stickers on the side of the plane.

Step 6: Glue and hammer the wheels.

I recommend everybody at The Prestige School to join Home Depot workshops.

My Winter Vacation

I went to Miami by plane. It was a three hour flight. When we got to Miami, we went to the cruise by taxi. It was a huge, black Disney cruise ship. The inside of the ship was beautiful. I saw Mickey and Minnie walking around and people were taking pictures with them.

Then I went to the Oceaneer Lab and I saw a big T.V. and many computer games. There were lots of kids playing there. I was driving the ship in the lab and I played games. It was fun.

There were lots of fun activities for kids on the ship. There was a big pool with slides on the top deck of the ship. The slide had twenty steps and I could see the pool from the top. When it was my turn, I waited for the green light and slid down on my belly. At the bottom of the slide I fell into the pool. It was so much fun. I got hungry and I ate two hotdogs with fries. I enjoyed it a lot. There was a movie playing in the pool area.

At nighttime, there was a pirate party and firecrackers at the top deck. I can’t wait to go on another cruise with my family.


Edmond's Book Review

Student of the Week from November 4 – 8, 2013

Puff the Magic Dragon
by Peter Yarrow and Lenny Lipton

This book is about a dragon. His name is Puff. He is a magic dragon who lives by the sea. Puff meets Little Jackie Paper and they become friends. They like to play on the water and have fun together.

My favourite part is when Puff and Little Jackie Paper are in a boat and see dolphins. This is my favourite part because I like dolphins. I do not like when Puff is alone because he is sad.

I would recommend this book to a friend because it is a good book. I give Puff the Magic Dragon five stars.

Student of the Week from September 9 - 13, 2013

I went to LegoLand and I went on the rides. Then I went on the roller coaster and I shot all the targets. I saw the CN Tower and I saw a soccer game that was made of Lego.

I went to Blue Mountain and I went to the swimming pool. I enjoy diving. Then I went to paint a muffin. I went to a place and made a gingerbread house and put icing on top and ate it.

I went to African Lion Safari and I saw a monkey. He jumped on our car. I saw a baby monkey hanging from her mommy’s tummy and drinking milk. That was interesting for me.
