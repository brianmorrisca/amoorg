Ryan's Movie Review

Student of the Week from June 2 – 6, 2014

The Lego Movie

Characters: The main characters are Emmet, Batman, the bad cop, and Lucy.

Setting: The setting takes place in the Lego Universe.

Plot Summary: Emmet touched the “thing” when it said, “Do not touch!” Then Emmet fell into the portal and saw the man upstairs.

Favourite Part: My favourite part is when Emmet touched the “thing” because the “thing” was attached to his back. This made him special, which made me happy.

Favourite Character: My favourite character is Emmet because when he is given instructions, he must listen to them and follow them. I like this because he does not make mistakes when he is building.

Rating: I would give this movie five stars because I like how Emmet is the special construction worker and I like the couch he made.

Daniel's Movie Review

Student of the Week from May 12 – 16, 2014

Percy Jackson: Sea of Monsters

Characters: The main characters are Percy Jackson, Luke, Hermes, Thalia, Clarisse, and Annabeth.

Setting: The setting takes place in the woods and the ocean.

Plot Summary: It was a dark night and Annabeth was running in the woods. Then she saw Cyclops appear and kill Thalia. Then Zeus found a new way for her to live. The tree was not an ordinary tree because it made a border to keep the special children safe from monsters.

Then the children were competing in an event called “Knockout”. At the battle Percy was winning, but Clarisse was not far behind. Then suddenly everyone heard a noise that blew the border and broke it. Percy and his friends went to the sea of monsters to bring back the Golden Fleece to protect the special land all the children live in.

Favourite Part: My favourite part is when Percy Jackson and the others were battling the master bad guy. I like this part because it had a lot of action.

Favourite Character: My favourite character is Percy Jackson because he never gives up on people and he helps them.

Rating: I would give this movie five stars because it is funny, scary, and full of action.


Edmond's Movie Review

Student of the Week from May 5 – 9, 2014

Shrek the Third

Characters: The main characters are Shrek, Fiona, Donkey, Puss in Boots, Prince Charming, and Artie.

Setting: The setting takes place in Shrek’s swamp and in the land of Far, Far Away.

Plot Summary: Fiona’s dad died because he was old. Then the kingdom had to get a new king. Shrek, Donkey, and Puss in Boots went to find Fiona’s cousin, Artie at a high school assembly. Then they all went to Mrs. Merlin’s house. At night Shrek, Donkey, and Puss in Boots went camping.

Favourite Part: My favourite part is when Shrek dreamed about having trillions of ogre babies while he was on the boat. I like this part because it was funny watching Shrek with so many babies.

Favourite Character: My favourite character is Artie because he killed Prince Charming. Then he became king and the villains became Artie’s friends.

Rating: I would give this movie five stars because it’s interesting, funny, and exciting.
